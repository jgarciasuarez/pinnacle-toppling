README (2/8/21)

The two ascii files (.V2 and .V3) in this folder are accelerations and various spectra from the Christmas Canyon strong motion sensor. 

They document the M7.1 Ridgecrest earthquake at 03:19:53 UTC on July 6.

Downloaded from the Center for Engineering Strong Motion Data on Feb 8 2021.

35.5249 N 117.3645 W, in soft rock.
